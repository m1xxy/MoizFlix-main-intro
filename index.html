Enjoy Ad-Free Streaming

Moizflix uses third-party player servers. To block annoying pop-ups and video ads in the App or Website, follow this one-time network trick on your phone.

Private DNS Setup

Private DNS provider hostname
Enter this exactly dns.adguard.com
1

Open Device Settings

Go to your Android phone's Settings and navigate to Connections or Network & Internet.

2

Find Private DNS

Tap on More Connection Settings (if applicable) and select Private DNS.

3

Enter the Hostname

Choose "Private DNS provider hostname", type in dns.adguard.com, and tap Save.

Once saved, restart your app/browser. All third-party video ads and pop-ups will be permanently blocked!
